About Lucas Pereira

A World Seen Through the Lens

The Early Years in Brazil

Lucas Pereira’s journey into photography began in the vibrant landscapes of his homeland, Brazil. As a child, he was captivated by the changing light on the dense Atlantic forests and the shimmering waves along the coast. With an old family camera, he learned to frame the world around him — capturing the raw energy of street festivals, the serenity of hidden trails, and the warmth of everyday life. Those early years planted the seeds for a lifelong pursuit of capturing the world’s beauty through a lens.

Exploring the World Beyond

Driven by a restless curiosity, Lucas's photography has taken him far beyond Brazil’s borders. He has traversed icy fjords in Scandinavia, wandered through golden deserts in the Middle East, and stood in awe before towering mountain ranges across the globe. Each destination presented a new challenge and a unique story to tell. Yet for Lucas, the journey isn’t about ticking off locations — it’s about immersing himself in the fleeting moments that make a place unforgettable: a village market bustling at dawn or a tranquil shoreline kissed by the last light of day.

The Philosophy Behind the Lens

For Lucas Pereira, photography is a meditation on life’s beauty and impermanence. He believes that every photograph should invite viewers to see the world anew — to pause, reflect, and reconnect with nature’s rhythms. His work isn’t about capturing perfection but about honouring the authentic and often overlooked details: the texture of windswept grass, the play of shadows on a rocky cliff, or the gentle dance of leaves in a forest breeze. “The camera,” Lucas says, “isn’t just a tool; it’s a bridge between my experience and yours.”

“The world moves fast — but through a lens, time slows, and beauty reveals itself in unexpected places.”

— Lucas Pereira
